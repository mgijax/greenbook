Figure 17-3. Reproductions of paintings by Marcia L. Craig of cells from mouse blood smears. Erythroid precursor cells from the hematopoietic liver of a 14-day C57Bl/6 fetus, arranged according to presumed order of maturation: proerythroblast (A), basophilic erythroblasts (B, C, D), polychromatophilic erythroblast (E), normoblast (F), and reticulocyte (G) (Wright-Lepehne stain).
