Figure 21-1. Coat colors of mice.
 
A. Yellow ( Ay); phaeomelanin.   B. Agouti (+/+); typical agouti banding.
 
C. Agouti chinchilla ( cch/ cch); note drastic reduction in phaeomelanin.   D. Nonagouti ( a/ a); pigment principally eumelanin except for a few hairs around mammae, ears, and perineum.
 
E. Black-and-tan ( at/ a); note regional differences in pigmentation, i.e., ventrum tan (yellow), dorsum black.   F. Nonagouti brown ( a/ a b/ b); pigment change from black to brown.
 
G. Nonagouti himalayan ( a/ a ch/ ch); thermolabile tyrosinase, pigmentation on extremities only.   H. Nonagouti pink-eyed dilution ( a/ a p/ p); drastic reduction of eumelanin.
