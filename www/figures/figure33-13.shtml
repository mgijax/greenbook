Figure 33-13. Inheritance of exploratory behavior in F1 hybrid mice. The diagonal is the "midpoint line," i.e., a line based on the mean scores for parental stocks plotted on both axes. Scores of F1 hybrids are arranged along the abscissa over the appropriate midparent value. The mean score for the hybrid is indicated along the ordinate. Note that most hybrids scored above the midparent line, and that the mean of the 18 hybrids whose parents were related (bull's-eye) is less than that of the 13 hybrids whose parents were unrelated (cross). (From Bruell, 1964a, with permission of the author and the American Zoologist.)

Bruell, J.H. 1964a. Inheritance of behavioral and psychological characters of mice and the problem of heterosis. Amer. Zool. 4: 125-138.
