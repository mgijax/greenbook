Figure 21-2. Coat colors of mice.
 
A. Albino ( c/ c); no pigment.   B. Nonagouti brown dilute piebald spotting ( a/ a b/ b d/ d s/ s); recessive type of spotting due to s allele; pigmented areas diluted because of d allele.
 
C. Nonagouti white ( a/ a Miwh /+); dominant spotting especially on ventrum, reduction of hair pigment in pigmented hairs.   D. Nonagouti viable dominant spotting ( a/ a Wv/+) ( Chapter 17, pleiotropism).
 
E. Nonagouti patch viable dominant spotting ( a/ a Ph Wv/+ +); hyperadditive interaction between Ph and Wv.   F. Varitint-waddler ( Va/+); variegation (mosaicism) not due to X-chromosome inactivation.
 
G. Nonagouti tortoise ( a/ a To/+); variegation attributed to X-chromosome inactivation.   H. Cattanach translocation. Segment of chromosome I inserted in the X chromosome; variegation attributed to X-chromosome inactivation.
